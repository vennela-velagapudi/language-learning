Spanish for Beginners

Start your journey of learning Spanish with the basics of the language, from pronunciation to vocabulary and grammar essentials.

Alphabet & Pronunciation

Vowels: a, e, i, o, u (ah, eh, ee, oh, oo)
Special letters: ñ, ll, rr

Essential Phrases

Hola = Hello
Buenos días = Good morning
Buenas tardes = Good afternoon
Buenas noches = Good night
Gracias = Thank you
Por favor = Please
De nada = You're welcome
¿Cómo estás? = How are you?
Muy bien = Very well
Adiós = Goodbye

Numbers 0-10

0 = cero
1 = uno
2 = dos
3 = tres
4 = cuatro
5 = cinco
6 = seis
7 = siete
8 = ocho
9 = nueve
10 = diez

Personal Pronouns

Yo = I
Tú = You (informal)
Él/Ella = He/She
Nosotros = We
Ellos/Ellas = They

Basic Verbs (Present Tense)

Ser = To be (permanent)

Yo soy = I am
Tú eres = You are
Él/Ella es = He/She is

Estar = To be (temporary)

Yo estoy = I am
Tú estás = You are
Él/Ella está = He/She is

Tener = To have

Yo tengo = I have
Tú tienes = You have
Él/Ella tiene = He/She has
