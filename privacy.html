Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to protect your personal data.

1. Information We Collect: We collect information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, phone number, and any other details you provide when creating an account or contacting customer support. Additionally, we gather information about how you interact with our platform, including the lessons you complete, your learning preferences, and any feedback you provide. We may also collect technical information, such as your IP address, device type, browser version, and operating system, to optimize your experience and troubleshoot issues. Cookies and similar tracking technologies are used to enhance functionality and gather analytics about user behavior.

2. How We Use Your Information: Your data helps us tailor the learning experience to your specific needs by offering personalized recommendations, progress tracking, and customized content. We analyze user activity to identify popular features, address areas needing improvement, and enhance overall performance. Additionally, your information is used for customer support, allowing us to quickly resolve issues and respond to your inquiries. In some cases, aggregated and anonymized data may be used for research purposes to improve language learning methodologies.

3. Data Sharing: We do not sell your personal information to any third parties. However, we may share your data with trusted third-party service providers, such as cloud storage solutions, analytics platforms, and customer support tools, to deliver a seamless user experience. These providers are contractually obligated to keep your information confidential and use it solely for the purposes of supporting our services. In rare cases, we may be required to share your information with legal authorities if it is necessary to comply with applicable laws or regulations.

4. Security: We employ a combination of physical, technical, and organizational measures to safeguard your personal information. This includes the use of encryption protocols, secure data storage systems, and routine security audits to detect and mitigate potential vulnerabilities. Our servers are housed in secure data centers with restricted access, and our team is trained in best practices for data protection. While no system is entirely foolproof, we continuously update our technology and processes to stay ahead of emerging security threats.

5. Your Rights: As a user, you have complete control over your personal data. You can access your information through your account settings and make updates or corrections at any time. If you wish to delete your account or request a copy of your data, our support team is available to assist you. We also provide options to manage your communication preferences, including opting out of promotional emails. Should you have any concerns about data handling or privacy practices, we encourage you to reach out, and we will promptly address your inquiries.

By using our platform, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our services, practices, or legal requirements. We encourage you to review it regularly to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.
