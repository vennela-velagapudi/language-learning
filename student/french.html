French for Beginners

Start your journey of learning French with the basics of the language, from pronunciation to vocabulary and grammar essentials.

Alphabet & Pronunciation

Vowels: a, e, i, o, u, y
Special characters: é, è, ê, ë, ç, â, î, ô, û
Silent letters rule
Liaison concept

Essential Phrases

Bonjour = Hello
Au revoir = Goodbye
S'il vous plaît = Please
Merci = Thank you
De rien = You're welcome
Comment allez-vous? = How are you?
Très bien = Very well
Excusez-moi = Excuse me
Je ne comprends pas = I don't understand
Parlez lentement = Speak slowly

Personal Pronouns

Je = I
Tu = You (informal)
Il/Elle = He/She
Nous = We
Vous = You (formal/plural)
Ils/Elles = They

Basic Verbs (Present Tense)

Être = to be

Je suis = I am
Tu es = You are
Il/Elle est = He/She is

Avoir= to have

J'ai = I have
Tu as = You have
Il/Elle a = He/She has

Aller = To go

Je vais = I go
Tu vas = You go
Il/Elle va = He/She goes
